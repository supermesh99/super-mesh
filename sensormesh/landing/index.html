01 — THESIS

Civilization runs on measurements nobody can verify.

FIG.01 / TOPOLOGY
Fig. 01 — Mesh topology, node adjacency under 0.16 radius

Coverage is sparse. Municipal air-quality stations sit kilometres apart while pollutant concentration varies block by block. The data that drives public-health policy is interpolated from a handful of points.

Provenance is absent. Insurers price climate risk, researchers publish findings, and cities levy regulation on feeds from operators whose incentives they cannot audit and whose readings they cannot reproduce.

Falsification is free. A centralized network can fabricate, censor, or backdate any record — and there is no cryptographic trail to prove it happened.

SensorMesh inverts the incentive: every reading is signed by a hardware key, backed by slashable stake, and exposed to open challenge before it settles. Honesty becomes the only profitable strategy.

02 — PROTOCOL

Optimistic settlement for physical truth.

Honest data is nearly free to submit. Disputes are the rare, expensive path — and every byte is attributable to a physical device.

I.
Register
A device's hardware key and its operator's wallet co-sign registration. The operator posts stake — slashable collateral held by the network treasury.
register_device
II.
Attest
The sensor signs each measurement. Raw payloads persist off-chain; a SHA-256 commitment and scaled value settle on Solana, rate-limited per device.
submit_reading
III.
Challenge
During the settlement window, any party may bond capital against a suspect reading. Frivolous challenges forfeit the bond to the device.
challenge_reading
IV.
Settle
Proven fraud slashes twenty percent of device stake to the challenger and burns reputation. Unchallenged readings finalize permissionlessly and accrue rewards.
finalize_reading
